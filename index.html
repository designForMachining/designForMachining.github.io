Design Optimization for CNC Machined Parts

Input your loading conditions, your machining workflow, your performance and lead time goals. Recieve an editable part, machining program, setup sheet, and performance metrics.

Manufacturing ease and speed as an objective

The common design optimization tools now incorporate constraints for machinability. The shortcoming of these tools is that they don't take into account the ease and speed of machining. These are very important factors in design. Soon, we will offer a tool which optimizes a design not just for performance but for conformity to simple repeatable CNC machining workflows and short lead times.

Form follows more than function

In reality, form also needs to follow the applied manufacturing method.
